# 360schoolERP Mobile — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2026

This policy describes how 360schoolERP mobile applications collect, use, and protect information for students, parents, and school staff.

## Apps covered

- **360schoolERP Family** (student & parent app)
- **360schoolERP Staff** (teacher, principal, transport app)

## Information we collect

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Admission number / employee code + registered mobile | Account authentication |
| OTP codes (when enabled) | Secure sign-in via SMS |
| Student academic data (attendance, homework, fees, etc.) | School services you access |
| Push notification tokens | Alerts from your school |
| Device type & app version | Reliable notifications |
| GPS location (staff transport app only, during duty) | Live vehicle tracking & safety |

We do **not** sell personal data to third parties.

## Children's data

The Family app displays information about enrolled students as provided by the school. Parents authenticate using mobile numbers on file with the institution. Schools are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents/guardians.

## Location data (transport staff)

When transport staff enable GPS tracking, location is sent to the school's server during configured duty hours for route monitoring, collision/deviation alerts, and emergencies. Location is not collected when tracking is stopped.

## Data storage & security

- Passwords are hashed; API traffic uses HTTPS.
- Session tokens are stored in the device secure keychain.
- Files uploaded (e.g. leave certificates) are stored on the school's backend.

## Third-party services

Depending on school configuration:

- **MSG91** — SMS OTP and absent alerts
- **Razorpay** — fee payments (parents)
- **Expo / FCM** — push notifications

## Your choices

- You may sign out at any time from Profile.
- Push notifications can be disabled in device settings.
- Contact your school to update registered mobile numbers or request data correction.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact your school administration or the 360schoolERP system administrator.
